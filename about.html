Meet Us

Antique “ through the years, memories still timeless”, Our product is made from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain coffee species. The coffee beans that we use are Arabica and Robusta that will serve as instant coffee granules, because they contain more than 100 chemicals including aromatic molecules, proteins, starches, oils, and bitter phenols, each contributing different characteristics to the unique flavor of the coffee. We also use hot brew coffee, steamed milk foam, and Hot milk to give an essential look to the top of our coffee. We made different kinds of coffee such as Iced Coffee, Dalgona Coffee, and Regular Coffee, with its different flavor.
